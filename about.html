About

I'm a Nigerian based graphic designer & brand identity designer who took his passion for design too serious. I've been fortunate to have had a rewarding career in design so far. I started my design career at Eximia Design Studio, then as a designer for the Lagos State Government, after which I worked at Mellicell, Planet3R and eventually at Partna (YC W22) where I grew from the role of graphic designer to Head of Brand.

I'm currently really interested in marketing and recently started Autobuddy as a result. I'm also contributing to Augmented Reality Interface design as a side hobby. I'm a Chelsea fan (Arsenal & Man Utd suck) and also recently became a follower of Jesus!

Other interests are learning about History, Philosophy and Gaming. My favourite games are Cyberpunk 2077, Red Dead Redemption 2 and my favourite movies as of today are Dune, LOTR and Pirates of the Caribbean.
